ARRK Pools are Cardano stake pool operators. Together, we secure rewards for delegators while researching efficent methods for future stake pool operators. ARRK operators believe that the way to grow and secure the Cardano ecosystem is to encourage diversified stake pool operations. ARRK pool operators will be documenting and video recording different methods of stake pool operations. Through this website and other online social media platforms, we will share our research and methods to those who wish to become Cardano stake pool operators.
